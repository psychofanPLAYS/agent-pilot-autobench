# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Agent Pilot Autobench to be a welcoming, practical project for people at every
experience level. Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback,
and keep discussions focused on improving the software and its evidence.

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and sharing
another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other spaces
where someone is representing the project.

## Reporting and Enforcement

For ordinary moderation concerns, contact the maintainers through a private
[security advisory](https://github.com/psychofanPLAYS/agent-pilot-autobench/security/advisories/new)
and mark the report as a conduct concern. Do not post sensitive details publicly.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when behavior threatens the safety or usefulness of the community.

## Attribution

This policy follows the principles of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1.
